## Researcher

<p class="orcid-link"><a href="https://orcid.org/0009-0002-1794-4895" target="_blank" rel="noopener">0009-0002-1794-4895</a></p>

**Zenodo community**: [Anthropie Working Papers](https://zenodo.org/communities/anthropie-working-papers)

**Theoretical framework** — Anthropy is the hypothesis that social systems displace disorder rather than resolve it. The framework of anthropy makes it possible to analyse mechanisms of transfer across spatial, temporal, and social dimensions.
