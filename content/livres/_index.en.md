The two books of the anthropic framework form the long-form foundation of the project. Where the *Anthropie Working Papers* formalise the hypothesis and apply it to specific objects — public debt, the energy transition, independent research —, the books deploy it across a broader arc: long-range history, economics, public finance, epistemology. Each book can be read independently; together, they map the full architecture of the framework.

The other works belong to a separate editorial project, prior to or running alongside the anthropic framework. Both books are published in French.
