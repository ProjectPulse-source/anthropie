The *Anthropie Working Papers* (AWP) constitute the academic corpus of the framework of anthropy. Each paper is deposited on [Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/communities/anthropie-working-papers) with a permanent DOI.

The series follows an arc: AWP-01 founds the hypothesis and its vocabulary; AWP-02 deploys it over the long run, across seven historical configurations; AWP-03, AWP-04, and AWP-06 apply it to three contemporary terrains — public debt, the energy transition, digital infrastructures; AWP-05 turns it back on the academic field itself; AWP-07 closes the arc by formalising the anthropic loop and situating the framework within its lineages — Kapp, Georgescu-Roegen, Stiegler. Each paper is published under a CC-BY 4.0 licence, with an English version carrying its own DOI and citation exports (BibTeX, RIS, EndNote). The framework formalised here is developed in the book [*ANTHROPIE — Ordre ici. Dette ailleurs*](/livres/anthropie-ordre-ici-dette-ailleurs/) (in French).
